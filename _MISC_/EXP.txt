XP is gained after battles. Each monster killed gives 1 XP Point. XP is only awarded on a completed, succesful battle.

XP can be used to upgrade your Team Stats. Team Stats apply to everyone on the team.

Vitality = Everyone gets +10 Health.
Sorcery = Everyone gets +20 Mana.
Charisma = Shopkeepers get 1 extra item.
Wealth = +10 Gold after a battle.
Stamina = Higher chance to dodge an attack.
